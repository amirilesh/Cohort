# Cohort Survey – Experiment Brief

## Decision this survey informs
Should we continue with Cohort MVP as proposed, and which features should be MVP priorities?

## Primary risk
People won’t use the app / don’t perceive enough value.

## Hypotheses (testable)
H1: Target users (students / curious learners) find the concept useful (avg usefulness ≥ 7/10).
H2: Target users show usage intent (≥ 60% rate 8–10 on “use weekly”).
H3: Trust is adequate (avg trust ≥ 7/10).
H4: Translation matters for a meaningful subset (avg ≥ 6/10 among non-native speakers).

## Target audience
Constructor students, friends

## Survey link + date range
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfXV4ZT-PFiWDOOtsXAI34mND77osJVKFt4t089S9mGFKqOeA/viewform?usp=header
## Metrics we will compute
- Mean usefulness (1–10)
- Mean weekly-use intent (1–10) + % high-intent (8–10)
- Mean trust (1–10)
- Top pain points (frequency)
- Feature priority rankings (avg)

## Decision rules
- Go: % high-intent ≥ __% in target segment AND usefulness avg ≥ __/10
- Pivot: usefulness high but trust low OR pain points mismatch
- Stop: low intent + low usefulness in target segment

## Notes / limitations
Sampling bias, self-report bias, small sample, etc.
